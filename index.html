Key Findings

Tax Burden Evolution

Chinese dynasties maintained relatively stable tax burdens of 7-10% GDP, comparable to contemporary Rome but lower than medieval Europe.

Tax Type Distribution

Land taxes dominated early dynasties, while commercial taxes became increasingly important in Song and later periods.
